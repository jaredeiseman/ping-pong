Ping-Pong

App Details

  • All numbers divisible by 3 will be replaced by "ping"
  • All numbers divisible by 5 will be replaced by "pong"
  • All numbers divisible by 15 will be replaced by "ping-pong"
  • Results will be displayed in the left hand column by order decided in first form option
